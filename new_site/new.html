Welcome

Red Junction is located in the Pacific Northwest at the foot of Mount Baker. We are committed to building locally, and building the best powder skis for those who love to ski or ride.

We are focused on selling in small quantities to ensure we build the highest quality products. If you are interested in our products, please contact us.

About

  • Our company

Are we using narwhal horn in your cores? Thankfully, no narwhals need to be injured to produce our products. Majority of the skis made are using similar technical materials and are composited in a similar manner. It is how the ingredients get combined and who combines them that make the difference. In the pursuit of the perfect ride, we believe craft, attention to detail, a youthful passion for the sport and the dedicated support of the community is the difference. It’s about being a ski/board company and not an image company.

We hand pick the core materials, laminate our own cores from continuous wood lengths, precisely cnc our materials, meticulously lay-up the product, and prep the ski/board to be ride ready on the first day of purchase. We offer various graphics created by local artists and designers (see artists bios) for each model we make so you can always find something that meets your aesthetic. Whether or not you are standing in a lift line, skinning a glacier or immersed in the white room you’ll love your skis or snowboard.

All skis/boards are crafted at our shop at the foot of Mt Baker. We support the local ski resorts, businesses, and avalanche centers and encourage you to do so. We don't produce your skis offshore we produce small quantities so we can maintain high quality.

We are passionate about three things:

  • Crafting the highest quality products in the industry with the most unique designs, creating something uniquely yours through personalization
  • Building locally and supporting the community
  • Being fanatical about skiing and riding

Our mission is to provide a superior crafted product locally that will bring your ‘woot’ out.

Who are we? We’re skiers and snowboarders passionate about riding the backcountry and deep powder so we make powder specific products. Collectively we have over 30 years of product design experience.

Dan Dole, a product designer for 18 years, started a company after an observation he had in 2007. He noticed how many people were leaving the resort and going into the backcountry. His wife had been riding a splitboard since 2000 and used to be the only boarder that he would see the backcountry but that had changed; more skiers and boarders were accessing the backcountry or slackcountry. This seems to be the biggest trend for skiing and snowboarding since, well, snowboarding and as good a time as any to get into the game.

In 2010, Dan opened Red Junction. He has spent the last three years doing research and development in order to establish a consistent and repeatable process that will produce the highest quality product.

Behind the scenes, Jennifer Day (Dan’s wife) has been working on the visual design and marketing materials for Red Junction. Red Junction’s mission wouldn’t be possible without the support of friends, family and the local community.
